A basic DAW for sound effects and music production. Would recommend using it with a connected keyboard or mouse, as drag and keyboard shortcuts play a major role here.  

Uses CLAP and LV2 plugins (not on fdroid) - no built-in effects to keep it minimal and lightweight.

Features:

- Multi-track audio recording and editing
- MIDI input with piano roll editor
- Plugin hosting (CLAP/LV2 instruments and effects)
- Automation lanes for parameter control
- Timeline-based arrangement
- Mixer with track controls
- WAV import/export
- Cross-platform: Linux, Windows, macOS, Android
- Lightweight, is under 20MB in apk size.
